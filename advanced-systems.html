Advanced Composites

Superior to the sum of their parts

Advanced composites combine two or more distinct constituents to create a material with properties superior to its individual components — often demanding specialised machinery for their complex manufacturing.

PMC

Polymer Matrix Composites

Reinforcing fibres bound within a polymer resin, valued for a high strength-to-weight ratio in airframe and UAV structures.

MMC

Metal Matrix Composites

A metallic matrix reinforced with a second phase, combining stiffness and thermal stability for demanding structural roles.

CMC

Ceramic Matrix Composites

Ceramic fibres in a ceramic matrix, engineered to withstand extreme temperatures with improved toughness.
